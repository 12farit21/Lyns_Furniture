Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Lyn's Furniture Miami ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using our website (lynsfurniture.net) or placing an order with us, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you:

  • Place an order (full name, email address, phone number, delivery address)
  • Contact us via email or phone
  • Browse our website (pages visited, time spent, browser type, IP address via cookies and server logs)

We do not collect or store payment card data directly — all payments are processed through third-party payment providers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Contact you about your order status or delivery
  • Respond to your questions and customer service requests
  • Improve our website and product offerings
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

3. Cookies

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to improve your browsing experience. Cookies help us remember your shopping cart, understand how visitors use our site, and deliver a better experience.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function (e.g., shopping cart, CSRF security token)
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors interact with the site (anonymized)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the site (such as the shopping cart) may not work properly without them.

4. Data Sharing

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our business, including:

  • Delivery and logistics companies (for order fulfillment)
  • Payment processors (for transaction processing)
  • IT and hosting providers (for website operation)

These providers are contractually required to keep your data confidential and use it only to provide services to us.

We may also disclose your information when required by law or to protect our legal rights.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Order records are typically retained for up to 7 years for accounting and legal purposes.

6. Your Rights (CCPA Notice)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) gives you certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Know — request information about the data we collect and how we use it
  • Right to Delete — request deletion of your personal data (subject to certain exceptions)
  • Right to Opt-Out — opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal data)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — we will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email below. We will respond within 45 days as required by law.

7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
